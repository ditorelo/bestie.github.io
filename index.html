Meet functional nutrition, your new BFF

Here’s the truth: you should feel good. It might be common to struggle with fatigue, headaches, gut issues, wacky periods, funky skin and shitty sleep, but it isn’t normal. You don’t have to feel this way. You’re not meant to feel this way.

Functional nutrition is for you if…

  • you’re feeling run-down, fatigued or foggy
  • you’ve got a list as long as your arm of foods that hurt your gut or give you IBS
  • the doctor says you’re fine but you’ve never felt worse
  • you’re struggling to get your energy back after having a baby
  • your periods are too heavy, too light or just too painful
  • your sex drive has vanished
  • you’re looking for natural ways to help the symptoms of autoimmune disease, anxiety, depression or adhd, asthma, eczema, breakouts or psoriasis
  • you’re stuck on a sugar and caffeine rollercoaster to get through the day
  • you’re preparing to get pregnant and want to give your body the best support possible
  • you’ve done the rounds with doctors, specialists and naturopaths and nothing has changed—or things changed for a while and then you were back where you started.

Meet functional nutrition, your new BFF

1. Mind + body

Mental health is physical health. Physical health is mental health. Your mind is part of your body — what happens in your body influences your mind, and your mind influences what happens in your body. You are one big physical, emotional, spiritual, mental system, made of stardust and powered by chips.

All those symptoms you’re battling are your body sending up red flags to let you know that things ain’t quite right inside. The good news is, every one of those red flags is a clue to what your body is crying out for — and bestie, my green flag is that I can’t resist a red flag 🚩

2. Food + feelings

Functional nutrition is not just a food plan or a supplement regime. You probably already know all the things you *should* be doing. You might even be doing them, and just not seeing results.

I can help you with the stuff behind the stuff — like figuring out if your body is absorbing the healthy food you’re feeding it, identifying hidden intolerances or sensitivities, figuring out how to make good habits stick or identifying where your subconscious beliefs or mindset are holding you back.

3. Science + magic

Listen, we know there’s no easy answer — your body is literally doing several billion things *a second.* By the time you’ve developed a health condition or are experiencing symptoms, there can be a lot to unpick. But there’s a rhyme and reason to why particular things go wrong, and together I promise we can ferret it out.

The best news is that we don’t have to guess. The Bestie secret sauce is the functional clinical assessment. It involves palpating various points on your body to assess for nutritional deficiencies — we cut out the middle man and ask your body what it needs directly. It’s not woo-woo, but it is more than a bit magic.

I’m… wildly nerdy about health, nutrition, mindset, energy, human design and more, good at explaining hard stuff, and passionate about helping you feel better.

You’re… ready to put in the work to see results, curious and open minded, and committed to becoming your best self.

Meet Katie

Feeling bad isn’t normal. You are not the problem. Modern life sets us all up to fail—but I want you to live your absolute best life.

I want to help you figure out what your best health looks like and how to get there—in a way that’s enjoyable, sustainable and lasts a lifetime.
